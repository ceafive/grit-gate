ABOUT US

Founded in Accra, Ghana in 2017, Grits Gate Ghana Limited is a Procurement, Supply and Logistics company. We focus on identifying and evaluating our clients’ needs in order to address their requirements with transparency and fluidity.

We have dedicated sourcing team whose expertise lies with the identification of suppliers, goods and services, the negotiation of pricing, terms and conditions and in the delivery to the end user.

We have in place systematic quality control process which provide our clients with the assurance of quality, personalized service and a complete client satisfaction. Our experience in the Logistics and Transport space offers a streamlined and synchronized solution to fulfill all our clients’ needs.
