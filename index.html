ABOUT US

Grits Gate Ghana Limited is a Procurement, Supply and Logistics company. We focus on identifying and evaluating our clients needs in order to address their requirements with transparency and fluidity.
We have dedicated sourcing team whose expertise lies with the identification of suppliers, goods and services, the negotiation of pricing, terms and conditions and in the delivery to the end user.
We have in place systematic quality control process which provide our clients with the assurance of quality, personalized service and a complete client satisfaction. Our experience in the Logistics and Transport space offers a streamlined and synchronised solution to fulfill all our clients needs.
To guarantee success in doing business with Grit Gate Ghana, we ensure the following:

  • Strategic Sourcing
  • Spend Analysis
  • Tactical Purchasing
  • Material Master Management and Data Classification
  • Supplier Data Management

Supply Chain

We have developed a strong network of partners for the whole supply value chain, this goes beyond trading and supply of cost effective items but also involves value added services such as end to end cost benefit analysis on sizing and selection of equipment.
We provide different options for cients to choose from and advise appropriately on long term benefits and effects of their choices. We have currently helped over 10 companies obtain value for money whenver they decide to purchase new equipment of replace existing ones.

Project Management

We offer project management services to produce a complete project which complies with the client's objectives. We also in many cases shape or reform the client's brief to feasibly be able to address the client's objectives.
We put in every endeavor inclined to produce a unique product, service or result with a defined beginning and end to meet unique goals and objectives, typically to bring about beneficial change or added value.
We use the four step approach namely:

  • Plan: The planning and forecasting activities
  • Process: The overall approach to all activities and project governance
  • People: Including dynamics of how they collaborate and communicate
  • Power: Lines of authority, decision-makers, organograms, policies for implementation and the like
